Stephen Curry

Summary

He is a American professional basketball player playing for the Golden State Warriors in the NBA.

Education

  • Graduated sociology major at Davidson-University, America

Work Experience

  • Basketball player at Davidson-University (2006-2009).

    Freshman season:

    • Curry finished the season leading the Southern Conference in scoring with 21.5 points per game.
    • Curry was named Southern Conference Freshman of the Year, SoCon Tournament MVP, and selected to the SoCon All-tournament team, All-freshman team, and All-SoCon First Team.

    Sophomore season:

    • He led the Wildcats to a 26–6 regular-season record and a 20–0 conference record. As a result of Curry's exceptional play, Davidson earned its third straight NCAA tournament bid.

    Junior season:

    • In his final season at Davidson, Curry averaged 28.6 points, 5.6 assists, and 2.5 steals.
    • Curry was named Southern Conference Freshman of the Year, SoCon Tournament MVP, and selected to the SoCon All-tournament team, All-freshman team, and All-SoCon First Team.
